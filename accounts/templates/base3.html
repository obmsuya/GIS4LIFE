GIS4life Certificate
When all the curriculum items of a course have been completed, a red or white trophy will appear on the course dashboard, signifying that the certificate of completion is ready. Certificates can be saved as a .pdf or .jpg file so that you can easily share your accomplishment. In addition, certificates can be shared on Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn directly from GIS4life.
Registration Process
  • 1. Sign in to see the list of available courses, click on your choice then click register.
  • 2. After registration click pay button to make the payment.
  • 3. Two hours later, your Course will be activated and ready to go.
  • 4. Click my course button to see your registered courses.
